About

I'm a second-year PhD student in linguistics at MIT, with focus on semantics, fieldwork, and more recently, experimental semantics.
I primarily work on Gitksan (Tsimshianic) and Japanese, and I'm especially interested in tense, aspect, modality and degrees.
Prior to MIT, I completed the MA program at University of British Columbia; my MA thesis was on tense semantics in Gitksan, Japanese, and English.

Please see the ``Research'' tab for publications.
